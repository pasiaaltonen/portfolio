Rise from Valhalla

Rise from Valhalla is a top down wave based shooter game where player controls a Viking shooting undead enemies. Here a nameless Viking has fallen in battle and made a deal with ancient gods that his family would be safe and live long lives. As he has accended to Valhalla he relizes that there has been a betrayal and an ancient demon has taken over Valhalla. Our Vikings only choise is to battle out his out of Valhalla by defeating the demon to save his family.


Game is in 2D top down view. All graphics have been hand painted. Currenntly there are 3 types of weapons that are droppend by enemies which player can use. Each weapon deal different amount of damage and has a different speed the ammunition moves. Goal is to defeat 10 waves of enemies with increasing difficulty to reach to the boss fight.
