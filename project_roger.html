Roger Powers
the Space Ranger

You are Captain Roger Powers the last remaining space ranger in the intergalactic war agains a dark warlord. Your objective in the game is to reach to the end of the level where the dark warlord awaits to encounter you in a final battle for the faith of the qalaxy.

Objective is to reach the end of the level and beat the high score. You score points by killing enemies. Different enemies gives you different amount of points.


Game is in 2.5D top down view space shooter. Enemies fly towards the player from the top of the screen.
