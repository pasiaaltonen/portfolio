About Me


Hi, I’m Pasi a third year game developer student living in Kaarina Finland.

I’m Currently studying information and communication technology batchelor's degree in Game and Interactive technology at Turku University of Applied sciences. My secret passion has always been video games and games in general but I have just recently started to make a career out of it. Making games is hard but rewarding and extremely fun.

Still my work history lies on business side as in the past ten years I’ve been working as accountant and after few years I moved to IT as a software tester. From here my passion to learn how all these applications that I have been using, games I have been playing actually work. So, here I am today feeling like a middle-aged teenager learning game design and programming and pushing my self to make a career out of something that before I never thought possible.

While not learning new skills on game development I spend time with music. Music has been the other mistress in my life as in listening and playing. As in games, music is all about touching people on the inside.
